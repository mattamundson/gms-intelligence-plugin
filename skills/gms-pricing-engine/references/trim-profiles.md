# GMS Trim Profiles Reference

## How to Use This File

This reference catalogs all **131 ACTIVE trim families** used by Greenfield Metal Sales for roofing, flashing, and trim applications.

### Key Concepts

- **Stretchout**: Inches of material required per foot of finished trim. Determines coil/flatsheet consumption via: `(Stretchout / Coil Width) × Coil Price × Length`
- **Bends**: Number of bends in the profile shape
- **Hems**: Number of hemmed edges (affects labor)
- **Material Pattern**: Whether trim uses 20-inch coils (Pattern A) or 41-inch material (Pattern B)
- **Gauge**: 24ga (CMG-only), 26ga, or 29ga. Pricing multipliers: 26ga = 29ga × 1.20 | 24ga = 29ga × 1.44

### Important Notes

- **HF Family Override**: All HF families use 41-inch material in production, with CMG colors sourced from `FS4126CMG{abbrev}` flatsheets
- **Color Premiums**: Crinkle (+15%), Matte (+8%), CMG vs US Standard (+19%) — NOT currently applied in Paradigm as of 2026-03-18
- **Panel Families**: 8 real panels exist (listed separately below). ONLY these are valid: Classic Rib, PBR, Pro Panel, FF100, SSQ550, SSQ675, Trapezoid, B&B

## PID Structure & Color System

### PID Format
```
{FAMILY}{GAUGE_DIGIT}{COLOR_CODE}{LENGTH}

Gauge digits:
  4 = 24ga
  6 = 26ga
  9 = 29ga

Examples:
  HFBT6MGALM12  = HF Bull Trim, 26ga, CMG Almond, 12ft
  BBJT9AW10     = BB J-Trim, 29ga, Alamo White, 10ft
  CO2026AG      = Coil 20-inch 26ga Ash Gray
  FS4126CMG-DKB = Flatsheet 41-inch 26ga CMG Deep Black
```

### Color Categories (166 total)
- **US Standard** (43 colors): Alamo White, Arctic White, Ash Gray, etc. — available 29ga/26ga (no 24ga)
- **US Crinkle** (18 colors): +15% premium — available 29ga/26ga (no 24ga)
- **US Matte** (7 colors): +8% premium — available 29ga/26ga (no 24ga)
- **US Woodgrain** (30 colors): 26ga only — specialty wood texture finishes
- **CMG Standard** (45 colors): Premium Kynar paint — 26ga/24ga (24ga only for select colors)
- **CMG Crinkle** (14 colors): CMG + crinkle — 26ga only
- **CMG Matte ULG** (7 colors): CMG + matte ultra-low gloss — 24ga only

> **Note**: Color premium multipliers (Crinkle x1.15, Matte x1.08, CMG x1.19) are documented but NOT yet applied in Paradigm pricing as of 2026-03-18.

## Quick Reference: Common Trim Profiles by Application

### Residential Roofing (Top Sellers)
- **J Trim** (JT): 5" stretchout, 2 bends, 1 hem — standard sidewall
- **Gable Trim** (GT): 10.25" stretchout, 4 bends, 2 hems — peak finish
- **Eave Trim** (EV): 5.75" stretchout, 2 bends, 1 hem — soffit edge
- **Ridge Cap** (RC): 12" stretchout, 6 bends, 0 hems — roof peak

### Commercial Buildings
- **Commercial Rib Base** (CRB): 6" stretchout, 3 bends, 1 hem — foundation
- **Com. Rib Rake & Corner** (CRRC): 17" stretchout, 5 bends, 2 hems — peak detail
- **Com. Rib Drip Flashing** (CRDF): 8" stretchout, 2 bends, 1 hem — water management

### Specialty / Premium
- **HF Sidewall Flashing** (HFSF): 6" stretchout, 41" material — high-end custom
- **HF Valley** (HFVF): 24" stretchout, 41" material — complex ridge integration
- **Band Board** (BB5): 12" stretchout, 0 bends, 0 hems — flat aesthetic finish

### Doors & Openings
- **Overhead Door Trim** (ODT8, ODT10): 14–16" stretchout, 5 bends — commercial doors
- **Sliding Door Trim** (SDT7, SDT9): 11–14" stretchout, 3 bends — multi-slide configurations
